Excellence in Another Area







In this year's iGEM, the concept of "excellence in another area" was proposed. This is a revelation for our work. iGEMers must not only make their own achievements in the scientific field, but also think about how to influence the world in many ways. We think this is a new requirement for human practical work.

Because of the impact of the COVID-19, team BIT begin to think about how to carry out related work. In 2020, China will comprehensively build a well-off society. The members of team BIT are also thinking about how to use their own strength to contribute to the country.

Therefore, the members of team BIT started from the work of human practice and thought. There is a saying in China, "Poverty Alleviation and Aspiration", which means that we must not only help poor people rely on material poverty, but also help them establish their ideals. The members of the BIT team thought of these words and thought of the human practice work advocated by iGEM. The members of the BIT team agreed that we can also use our expertise to help the poor.

In the past, team BIT often went to elementary and high school to give science lectures, but most of them are close to the school. But this year and in the future, team BIT hopes to give knowledge to children in more distant areas, to help them see the outside world, and let them establish a lofty ideal! Therefore, team BIT got in touch with the Escort, a club of Beijing Institute of Technology. They are a team of volunteers dedicated to supporting teaching activities. Team BIT got in touch with them, hoping to jointly promote synthetic biology promotion lectures in the future, and send knowledge and strength to more children who have no chance to contact relevant knowledge.

Due to the impact of the COVID-19, there is no opportunity for offline education support activities this year. However, we have reached a cooperation with the Escort, produced a series of synthetic biology-related postcards, and wrote letters to the children. Team BIT will continue to implement related work in the future, so that human practice has more diverse performances. Hope we do better in another area in the future!
